RECITALS

WHEREAS, Provider offers enterprise artificial intelligence solutions and related services; and

WHEREAS, Customer desires to engage Provider to deliver such services under the terms set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. SERVICES AND DELIVERABLES

1.1 Provider shall deliver AI-powered analytics platform services as described in the attached Statement of Work (SOW).

1.2 Provider will use commercially reasonable efforts to deliver services in accordance with the SOW timeline.

1.3 Any modifications to services require written approval from Provider's account management team.

2. TERM AND TERMINATION

2.1 Initial Term: This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of thirty-six (36) months ("Initial Term").

2.2 Renewal: This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least one hundred eighty (180) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

2.3 Termination for Convenience by Provider: Provider may terminate this Agreement at any time with sixty (60) days written notice to Customer.

2.4 Termination for Convenience by Customer: Customer may terminate this Agreement only with one hundred eighty (180) days written notice to Provider and upon payment of an early termination fee equal to 75% of the remaining contract value.

2.5 Termination for Cause: Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days of receiving written notice.

2.6 Effect of Termination: Upon termination, Customer shall immediately cease all use of Provider's services and return or destroy all Provider materials. All fees paid are non-refundable.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 Annual Fee: Customer agrees to pay Provider an annual fee of $3,200,000 for the services, payable in quarterly installments of $800,000.

3.2 Payment Terms: All invoices are due within fifteen (15) days of invoice date. Late payments shall accrue interest at 2% per month.

3.3 Fee Increases: Provider reserves the right to increase fees by up to 15% annually upon sixty (60) days written notice.

3.4 Additional Services: Any services beyond the scope of the SOW shall be billed at Provider's then-current rates.

4.2 Customer Data: Customer grants Provider a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, modify, and create derivative works from any data, content, or materials provided by Customer for the purpose of delivering services and improving Provider's platform.

4.3 Work Product: Any custom developments, configurations, or work product created under this Agreement shall be owned exclusively by Provider. Customer receives only a limited, non-transferable license to use such work product during the term of this Agreement.

4.4 Feedback: Any suggestions, feedback, or ideas provided by Customer regarding Provider's services shall become the sole property of Provider without any compensation to Customer.

4.5 Residual Rights: Provider retains all rights to use any residual knowledge, skills, or experience gained during the performance of services under this Agreement.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY

5.1 Confidential Information: Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other party's Confidential Information for a period of five (5) years following disclosure.

5.2 Exceptions: Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is publicly available through no breach of this Agreement; (b) was rightfully known prior to disclosure; or (c) is independently developed.

5.3 Provider's Use: Notwithstanding the above, Provider may use Customer's Confidential Information for internal analytics, benchmarking, and service improvement purposes.
