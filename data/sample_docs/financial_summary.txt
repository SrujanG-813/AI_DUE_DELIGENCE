TECHVISION AI - FINANCIAL SUMMARY
Q4 2025 Financial Overview

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

TechVision AI is experiencing exceptional growth in the enterprise AI solutions market. Our innovative platform has captured significant market share, and we project continued exponential expansion through 2026 and beyond.

REVENUE PERFORMANCE

Q4 2025 Revenue: $2.8 million
Year-over-Year Growth: 340%
Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR): $950,000

Our revenue growth has been nothing short of spectacular. We've onboarded 47 enterprise clients in Q4 alone, with an average contract value of $85,000 annually. The sales pipeline for Q1 2026 looks incredibly promising, with over $12 million in potential deals at various stages.

GROWTH PROJECTIONS

We are confident in our ability to achieve the following milestones:

- Q1 2026: $4.2 million revenue (50% quarter-over-quarter growth)

These projections are based on our current sales velocity and the massive untapped market opportunity. The enterprise AI market is expected to reach $500 billion by 2028, and we are perfectly positioned to capture a significant share.

Our burn rate is well-managed, and we expect to reach profitability by Q3 2026 based on current growth trajectories.

CUSTOMER METRICS

FUNDING & RUNWAY

We completed a Series A round of $15 million in August 2025, led by Apex Ventures. With our strong revenue growth, we are well-capitalized to execute our expansion plans.

The total addressable market for enterprise AI solutions is estimated at $85 billion annually. We are targeting the mid-market and enterprise segments, which represent approximately $45 billion of this opportunity. Our current market penetration is less than 0.01%, indicating massive room for growth.

COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES

- Proprietary AI algorithms with 40% better accuracy than competitors
- First-mover advantage in vertical-specific AI solutions
- Strong brand recognition in target markets

RISKS & CHALLENGES

While our growth trajectory is strong, we acknowledge some areas requiring attention:

However, we are confident these challenges are manageable and will not impede our growth momentum.

CONCLUSION

TechVision AI is at an inflection point. Our technology is proven, our market fit is validated, and our growth is accelerating. We are poised to become the dominant player in enterprise AI solutions within the next 24 months.

The financial fundamentals are strong, and we have the team, technology, and market opportunity to deliver exceptional returns to our investors.
