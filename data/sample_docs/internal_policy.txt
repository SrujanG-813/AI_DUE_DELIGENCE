SECTION 2: TECHNOLOGY INFRASTRUCTURE

2.1 CLOUD ARCHITECTURE

Our entire platform runs on Amazon Web Services (AWS). We have built our architecture specifically around AWS services including:

- AWS SageMaker for model training and deployment
- AWS Lambda for serverless compute
- AWS RDS for database management
- AWS S3 for data storage
- AWS CloudFront for content delivery

All application code is tightly integrated with AWS-specific APIs and services. Our engineering team has deep expertise in AWS technologies.

2.2 SCALABILITY INFRASTRUCTURE

TechVision AI has built a highly scalable, cloud-native architecture designed to handle unlimited growth. Our microservices architecture allows us to scale horizontally across multiple availability zones with automatic load balancing.

We can easily scale to support 10x our current customer base without any infrastructure changes. Our auto-scaling policies ensure optimal performance during peak usage periods.

The platform is designed to handle millions of concurrent users with sub-second response times. We have stress-tested the system up to 50,000 simultaneous API requests with no degradation in performance.

2.3 DISASTER RECOVERY

Backup Strategy: Automated daily backups to AWS S3
Recovery Time Objective (RTO): 4 hours
Recovery Point Objective (RPO): 24 hours

Dr. Sharma maintains the disaster recovery runbooks and is the designated recovery coordinator for all critical incidents.

SECTION 3: VENDOR RELATIONSHIPS

3.1 CRITICAL VENDORS

Amazon Web Services (AWS)
- Relationship: Primary cloud infrastructure provider
- Contract Term: 3-year commitment signed in January 2025
- Annual Spend: $1.68 million
- Services: Compute, storage, database, ML services
- Migration Complexity: High - extensive use of AWS-proprietary services

DataCore Analytics
- Relationship: Exclusive data processing partner
- Contract Term: 5-year exclusive agreement
- Annual Spend: $420,000
- Services: Data pipeline processing and transformation
- Proprietary Integration: Custom API integration built specifically for their platform

NeuralTech Solutions
- Relationship: AI model training infrastructure
- Contract Term: 2-year agreement with auto-renewal
- Annual Spend: $680,000
- Services: GPU cluster access for model training
- Dependency Level: Critical - all model training runs on their infrastructure

3.2 VENDOR RISK ASSESSMENT

AWS Dependency: While AWS provides excellent reliability, our architecture is deeply integrated with AWS-specific services. We have not evaluated multi-cloud strategies as our team's expertise is concentrated in AWS technologies.

DataCore Analytics: Our data processing pipeline is built entirely on DataCore's proprietary API. The integration took 8 months to develop and would be extremely difficult to replicate with another vendor.

NeuralTech Solutions: We do not maintain our own GPU infrastructure for model training. All training workloads are executed on NeuralTech's clusters. Building equivalent in-house capability would require $2-3 million in capital investment.

3.3 VENDOR ALTERNATIVES

We have not formally evaluated alternative vendors for our critical services. The engineering team believes that switching costs would be prohibitive and could take 12-18 months for a complete migration.

4.1 IP OWNERSHIP

All core AI algorithms and platform code are owned by TechVision AI, Inc. We maintain comprehensive IP protection through:

- 3 granted patents on neural network optimization techniques
- 5 pending patent applications
- Extensive trade secret protections

4.2 EMPLOYEE IP AGREEMENTS

All employees sign comprehensive IP assignment agreements upon hire. Any work product created during employment automatically becomes company property.

4.3 THIRD-PARTY IP

Our platform incorporates several open-source libraries and frameworks under various licenses (MIT, Apache 2.0, BSD). Legal review confirmed compliance with all license terms.

We license certain AI training datasets from DataCore Analytics under an exclusive agreement. These datasets are critical to our model performance and are not available from other sources.

4.4 CUSTOMER IP ARRANGEMENTS

Per our standard customer contracts, TechVision AI retains ownership of all platform improvements, custom configurations, and derivative works created during customer engagements. Customers receive only a limited license to use these materials during their contract term.

We also retain perpetual rights to use customer data for platform improvement, analytics, and benchmarking purposes. This data becomes part of our proprietary training datasets.
